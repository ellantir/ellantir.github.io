Biography

Fred Olaio, a Portuguese-born author, was destined for a life of creativity and imagination. Born in 1990, he discovered his passion for storytelling at a young age. After completing his studies in Chinese-Portuguese Translation and Interpretation, he embarked on a transformative journey to China, where he immersed himself in a vibrant culture and gained invaluable experiences.
Inspired by literary giants such as J.R.R. Tolkien, C.S. Lewis, and Philip Pullman, Fred's imagination took flight, and he dedicated himself to crafting his own mythical landscapes. In 2021, he achieved a significant milestone with the publication of his debut literary work in Portugal, captivating readers with his ability to transport them to extraordinary realms brimming with magic, adventure, and unforgettable characters.
Fred's creative pursuits extend beyond the realm of writing. Before venturing into the world of literature, he honed his artistic talents as a lead singer and guitarist, performing live music in Portugal and China. Additionally, he explored the realm of consulate work, gaining a diverse range of life experiences that fuel his storytelling.
As a seasoned traveler, Fred has visited over 40 countries, expanding his horizons and immersing himself in different cultures. This globe-trotting lifestyle has provided him with a wealth of knowledge and experiences, enriching his storytelling with authenticity and depth. Whether he's crafting new tales in a coffee shop somewhere around the world or working remotely for Chinese translation companies, Fred's digital nomad lifestyle allows him to pursue his passion for writing fantasy across various age groups.
